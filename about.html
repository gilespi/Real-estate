About Us

At The Real Estate, we’re on a mission to redefine hospitality by bringing exceptional stays everywhere.

>

Why we’re here

Hotels aren’t always hip. Hosts aren’t always reliable. So we’re changing the game—with spaces that inspire and delight, that fulfill needs without sacrificing style, all while delivering seamless, personalized experiences. Check in, request fresh towels, and get dinner recommendations—all from your phone.

How we got started

In the summer of 2020, founder managed several apartments. When he rented out his own, he greeted guests with wine, parked their cars and began to imagine a new set of ideals around hospitality. The following year, he created a startup that compared passengers to empty student apartments. That startup became The Real Estate Inc.

A better way to live

The Real Estate Inc. Today

Today, The Real Estate is an recognized hospitality brand. Headquartered in LA, CA, we offer studios, lofts, and apartments.

Where we’re going

What started as a journey for a better stay has grown into a celebration of modern design and exceptional comfort. We work with quality architects and designers to create truly exceptional spaces - ones that transcend hospitality and enrich life. And we use technology to make those spaces accessible while bringing The Real Estate Inc.
